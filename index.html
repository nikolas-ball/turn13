Building, Tuning, and Repair - We do it all.

We have spent many years tuning, repairing, and servicing all types of cars and motorcycles, everything from sport cars, superbikes, and Harleys. We are equipped with the latest and most advanced equipment such as a brand new Dynomometer from Dynojet to provide the best tuning for our customers. Oil changes, suspension rebuilds, carburetor and fuel-injection services, ECU Flashes, suspension re-valves, intake and exhaust modifications, or anything else you can think of, we can do for you.

If you love motorsports & outstanding customer service, then you will love our facility.

We Always Strive For Professionalism

Both owner/operators of the shop have extensive experience with customer satisfaction and motorsports. Having spent multiple years in schooling and formal training as well as years of servicing vehicles, you can be assured you are always getting the best quality.

We Have The Capability For Any Job

If your machine is not in need of an ECU Flash or Tune, then we can perform any other service or repair you may need. Are you thinking of an upcoming track day? We can get your car or bike race ready. Need new tires but you don't have the tools to do the job yourself? Give us a call.

We knew when the shop was being built that we wanted the absolute best Dynamometer (Dyno) that money could buy. Because we have this powerful machine in house, we have the ability to conduct far more than the typical power and torque measurements that other shops can do.
